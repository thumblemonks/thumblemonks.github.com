A ( Technology / Design / Media ) Collective

We're a collection of like-minded individuals who believe in doing the right thing for our clients.

We're a collective of like-minded individuals who also happen to be extremely talented media and technology professionals. Blah blah blah .... mention quality and fun, maybe something about our actual philosophy :/ We get together on projects, sit down to understand your needs, get it done right and quick, give back to the community. Doing the right thing! Incredibly agile with the right amount of structure (i.e. pragmatism)

When we say collective, we mean independence. Each of our members are free to work with whomever they want and you're free to get in touch with them however you please. Thumble Monks is simply a tool for harnessing the power of the group.

When we say collective, we mean we have a broad range of expertise; we do web application development, developed commercials, business process analysis and optimization, systems programming, architectural review, email expertise, information security, interactive marketing, ... . Our members come from a very diverse technology and media background: web, print, video, photography. In the event we don't represent a need you have, we'll help you find someone who does because it's in our mutual interest to do so.

We can do web application development and web design. If you're a design team and you need to find top notch developers, they're here. If you're top notch development team and need a designer, drop us a line. If you have an idea, but no team to design and develop, let us know. Whether your project is S, M, L, or XL ... we've seen it all. We're the yin to your yang. ...
