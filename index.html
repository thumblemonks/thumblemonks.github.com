A ( Programming / Web / Print / Video / Photography ) Collective

Thumble Monks is a collective of common sense individuals who also happen to be extremely talented media professionals.

When we say collective, we mean independence. Each of our members are free to work with whomever they want and you're free to get in touch with them however you please. Thumble Monks is simply a tool for harnessing the power of the group.

When we say collective, we mean we have a broad range of expertise. Our members come from all corners of mass media: web, print, video, photography. In the event we don't represent a need you have, we'll help you find someone who does because it's in our mutual interest to do so.
